## Hero Patterns

The locally bundled floor materials include recolored and layered derivatives of square,
repeatable SVG patterns by Steve Schoger. The app does not contact Hero Patterns at runtime.

- Source: https://heropatterns.com/
- Repository: https://github.com/sschoger/hero-patterns
- License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International

Changes include recoloring, opacity adjustment, square-tile composition, scaling, and layering.

## Lucide

Small seeded floor details use locally bundled Lucide React SVG icons. Their position, color,
rotation, and scale are controlled by the puzzle seed.

- Source: https://lucide.dev/
- Repository: https://github.com/lucide-icons/lucide
- License: ISC
